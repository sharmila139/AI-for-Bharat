# Minimum Viable Product (MVP).

App Title: RuralConnect AI (Suggested Name)  
Core Mission: Empowering rural communities through AI-driven insights in agriculture, healthcare, education, and infrastructure.

## Module 1: Smart Agriculture (Agri-Tech)

**Goal**: To optimize yield and sustainability through data-driven decisions.

### Crop Selection & Market Demand AI:

**Feature**: An AI recommendation engine that suggests crops based on real-time market prices and demand forecasts.

**Functionality**: Users input their location/season; the app analyzes market trends to suggest high-profit crops to plant.

### Soil & Water Analysis:

**Feature**: Digital Soil Health Card & Irrigation Planner.

**Functionality**: Users upload soil test photos or data. The AI interprets nutrient levels and suggests specific fertilizers. It also schedules watering based on local weather forecasts.

### Climate & Weather Integration:

**Feature**: Hyper-local weather alerts.

**Functionality**: Push notifications for rain, drought, or extreme weather, including preventative measures for specific crops.

### Sustainable Techniques:

**Feature**: Knowledge bank of organic farming and permaculture.

**Functionality**: Step-by-step guides on crop rotation and natural pest control to ensure long-term soil sustainability.

## Module 2: Primary Health Care (Tele-Health)

**Goal**: To provide immediate medical guidance and promote preventative health.

###  AI First Aid Assistant:

**Feature**: Interactive Symptom Checker & Emergency Guide.

**Functionality**: A chatbot or voice assistant guides users through basic first aid (CPR, cuts, burns, snake bites) with visual diagrams and video demonstrations before a doctor can arrive.

### Natural Medicine & Home Remedies:

**Feature**: Traditional Medicine Database.

**Functionality**: Verified natural remedies for common minor ailments (coughs, colds, digestion) based on locally available herbs.

### Lifestyle & Diet Tracker:

**Feature**: Rural Nutrition Planner.

**Functionality**: Customized diet plans based on local food availability and the physical labor intensity of the user (e.g., farming vs. weaving). Includes physical activity suggestions.

## Module 3: Education & Skill Development (Ed-Tech)

**Goal**: To bridge the literacy gap and provide vocational guidance.

### AI Video Tutor:

**Feature**: Adaptive Learning System.

**Functionality**: AI-generated or curated video classes that adapt to the student's pace.

### Multi-Lingual Support:

**Feature**: Real-time Translation & Voice Over.

**Functionality**: Content is available in the specific regional dialect/language, with an option to learn basic English as a second language.

### Career Guidance & Policy Finder:

**Feature**: Opportunity Scanner.

**Functionality**: A portal that matches users with relevant government schemes, subsidies, and scholarships based on their profile. It also suggests vocational career paths based on local industries.

## Module 4: Infrastructure & Civic Engagement

**Goal**: To crowd-source data for village development and accountability.

### Visual Grievance Redressal:

**Feature**: "Snap & Report" System.

Functionality: Users take photos of damaged roads, broken streetlights, or water leaks. The AI geo-tags the location and categorizes the issue automatically.

### Community Opinion Polls:

**Feature**: Village Voice.

**Functionality**: A voting system for residents to prioritize what infrastructure projects are needed most (e.g., "Do we need a new well or a school repair first?").

### Progress Dashboard:

**Feature**: Transparency Tracker.

**Functionality**: Users can view the status of reported grievances (Pending, In Progress, Resolved).
